The Story of AutoFeis



Introduction

AutoFeis was made solely by me, Spencer McMurray, a 19-year old Computer Science student at the University of Toronto. I've been dancing since I was 10, but been at feiseanna since I was 2 thanks to my sister who also dances.


Backstory

At all of the many feiseanna I've attended, results have always taken a long time to arrive. When I was younger I didn't understand why, but as I got older I started to see how much effort went into tabulation, and I grew much more respect for those who tabulate our results.


The Story

I've had an intrest in Computer Science for a long time, but only recently have I delved into the insane topic of Artificial Intelligence. It's far too complicated to explain to you in one paragraph, but I reccommend you search what goes into modern A.I if this intrests you. Anyway, I grew intrested in the idea of automating human tasks like driving cars and mastering games.

One day I was at a feis and I was curious to see how long it would take to tabulate our results. Two hours later we had our results, and I knew that A.I could speed up the process, while reducing the amount of human resources needed by a large amount.

It took many days full of programming, late nights, and early mornings, but I managed to complete it in just few months. So hopefully you can enjoy much more relaxed tabulation, along with knowing that the tech that went into this system may one day take over the world ;)
